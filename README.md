# 🛍️ Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Retail Sales Performance Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales data across different regions, retailers, products, and sales methods. It helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics, identify top-performing products, and evaluate profitability trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track overall sales performance and profitability.
- Analyze product-wise sales and operating profit.
- Compare sales across different sales methods.
- Monitor regional and retailer performance.
- Identify top-selling products and high-profit categories.

---

| KPI | Value |

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1. Operating Profit by Product
- Men's Street Footwear generates the highest operating profit.
- Women's Athletic Footwear contributes the lowest profit among all categories.
- Footwear categories dominate overall profitability.

### 2. Product Sales Analysis
- Men's Street Footwear is the top-selling product category.
- Women's Apparel ranks second in total sales.
- Men's Apparel records comparatively lower sales.

### 3. Operating Sales Distribution
- Product categories contribute almost equally to operating sales.
- Men's Street Footwear leads with approximately 18% share.
- Other categories contribute between 16% and 17%.

### 4. Sales by Sales Method
- **In-store Sales:** 40%
- **Outlet Sales:** 33%
- **Online Sales:** 27%

**Insight:** Physical stores contribute the majority of total sales.

### 5. Units Sold by Product
- Men's Street Footwear has the highest number of units sold.
- Women's Athletic Footwear records the lowest unit sales.
- Footwear products outperform apparel products in volume.

## 🔍 Key Business Findings

✅ Men's Street Footwear is the best-performing product in terms of sales, profit, and units sold.

✅ In-store purchases remain the preferred sales channel.

✅ Footwear categories contribute significantly more revenue than apparel categories.

✅ Sales distribution is balanced across product categories, reducing dependency on a single product segment.

✅ Regional and retailer filters help identify market-specific opportunities.
